My Hobbies

I have plenty of small hobbies. However, my main interests are mostly music related. I enjoy collecting vinyl records. I usually get them from the local record store or an antique shop near me that sells bundles of them. My collection isn't left to sit around, I use my records even if they are limited edition. Why buy them just to stare at them? My collection has plenty of Nat King Cole. He is by far my favorite artist. A close second would be The Moody Blues. I haven't heard a bad song from them yet. I collect a lot of music from the 20s-70s. Lesley Gore, Bernadette Carol, Ricky Nelson, The Ronettes, surprisingly no Elvis.

I also enjoy reading comic books. I know plenty about Batman. I used to collect comic books but they are so expensive, I read them online now. I play alot of batman video games aswell. Batman: Arkham Origins and Telltale Batman are my two favorites. I play other video games but I prefer storygames. Batman is the perfect game for those. Some of my favorite comics are:

  • Death of the Family
  • Batman: Zero Year
  • Batman: Europa
  • Joker: The Man Who Stopped Laughing

What I do in my Free Time

I enjoy practicing instruments. I have played clarinet for 7 years now. I enjoy clarinet alot. Playing it in marching band is the highlight of my year. When I get home, I try and play my piano, (which is actually just a keyboard,) atleast half an hour a day. However, I work as a cook at a bar in Antwerp, and usually I only have an hour or two at home before I go in for my shift. I also took guitar class and can play chords and tabs, however I cannot do bar chords, and strum patterns are a bit difficult sometimes.

When im not playing music or working, I like to spend my time riding on the trails in our woods with dirtbikes and our side-by-side. My sister and I spend hours out there sometimes. We have beautiful trails and would go out afterschool and come back at 10pm. We've put almost a thousand miles on the side-by-side.
