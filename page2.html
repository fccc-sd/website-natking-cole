My Pets

I have six pets that I love very much. Four of the animals are dogs, the other two are cats. My animals names are:

  • Sammy (dog)
  • Joy (dog)
  • Harley(dog)
  • Gunner(dog)
  • Jinx(cat)
  • Jax(cat)

We have had Sammy as long as I remember. Harley and Joy are her babies. Mom let both me and Grace keep one of the puppies. Joy is my dog and Harley is hers. Gunner is Sammy's grandson. One of the puppies we gave away had him. We drove all the way to virginia to get him. We chose him because he looked identical to Sammy. My cats are both strays. I actually just hid Jinx from dad until he heard him meow and Mom and I begged dad to keep him. He did. Same happened with Jax except I didn't hide him. Now he adores those cats and cuddles with them.

My Favorite books

I've always enjoyed reading. Especially classics. Most of my favorite books are classical books. I really like George Orwell. Animal farm and 1984 were mindblowing books to me when I first read them. The saddest book I ever read was Lolita. I bawled my eyes out for hours after I finished the book. It was insanely disturbing but so well thought out. You go the entire story knowing she was doomed from the start and that the story you are reading is warped through the eyes of a maniac. She never gets to tell her side of the story. I also love the Interview With The Vampire series. I've read all the books, watched the first movie, and watched the series. The series is wildly different from the book but they managed to make the plot even more deep and still keep the same essence of the books.

My Future Plans

I plan on going to Northwest after highschool and getting an associates. After that I will hopefully go to a bigger school like Trine or Toledo. My brother went to Trine, but engineering wasn't for him. My dream job is actually to be in the pit orchestras that travel the US with broadway shows. That would be a dream come true. However, music doesn't pay well and it's an extremely hard life to get into. You have to be very well experienced in multiple instruments since the style of the music and skill level changes with every show. Even though I most likely won't get to do this as a job, I am still going to involve myself in music the rest of my life through city bands parades, ect. It will forever be one of my favorite hobbies.
