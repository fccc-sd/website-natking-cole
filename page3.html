My Favorite Animals

My favorite domestic animals are cats. I think they are some of the best pets. Another one of my favorite pets to own is beared dragon. I owned one when I was younger and he was the best.

The animals I like that are not common pets would be racoons and deer. Racoons just look cool and deer are super pretty I love seeing baby deer when im riding in the woods.
